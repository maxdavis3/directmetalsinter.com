CAPABILITIES

DMLS Sintering Services

01

Powder Bed Fusion

Pre-alloyed powder (40-100μm) deposited in uniform layers. High-power laser selectively sinters each cross-section, building complex geometry layer by layer.

02

Thermal Management

Build chamber preheated to 400-600°C to minimize thermal gradients and residual stress — critical for Inconel 718 and titanium alloy structural integrity.

03

Support & Stress Relief

Support structures designed for efficient removal. Post-print stress relief at 650-950°C eliminates residual sintering stress before optional CNC finishing.

Sintering Science Applied

DMLS combines metallurgical precision with additive freedom — achieving densities indistinguishable from wrought material in any geometry.

  • ● Pre-alloyed powder for consistent final properties
  • ● Selective sintering — only desired geometry fused
  • ● Residual stress relief included as standard
  • ● HIP optional: 100% porosity elimination
  • ● Surface finish Ra 6-15μm as-sintered
  • ● CNC finishing to mirror quality on request
